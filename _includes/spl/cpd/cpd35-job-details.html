1. From the CPD main menu, click Deployments.
  2. In the Activity tab, click on View Active runs. This will take you to a list of job runs.
  3. In the Views drop down, choose Jobs with active runs.
  4. Click on the entry in the list matching your job name. If you submitted through the Streams Console. click the entry whose Type listed as Streams Legacy. Otherwise, click the entry matching the application name.

  5. From the list of job runs that appears, click the job run based on the job id and start time:

  6. This opens the Job Details page.

    • To open the Job Graph, click the Streams job graph link.

    • To download logs, click the Logs tab to create a log snapshot and download it.
